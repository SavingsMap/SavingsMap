How to Use the SavingsMap Calculator

SavingsMap is a free savings goal calculator that shows you exactly how long it will take to reach your financial goal — whether it's an emergency fund, vacation, car, house deposit, or anything else.

Simply enter your goal amount, your current savings, and how much you can save each month. Our calculator factors in compound interest and shows you a visual roadmap with milestones at 25%, 50%, and 75% of your journey.

Use the Boost Scenarios feature to see how saving a little extra each month can dramatically cut down your timeline. Even an extra $50/month can shave months off your savings journey.
